Download

Download the MCTest dataset, which includes answers for the training and development set. When you are ready, download the answers for the test set. We encourage you to wait until you are nearly ready to publish before downloading the test set answers.

We plan to periodically create new test sets to help avoid the inevitable problem that, over time, results may be overfitting the test set. We will also post new results on the Results page as they are published. We encourage you to check back occasionally to see if anything new has been added.

Auxillary Data

Here we will also post other data sources that you may find helpful, or may be needed to reproduce publisehd results.
  • Stopword List: Download the list of stopwords we used when computing the distance-based baseline
  • RTE statements: Download the MCTest questions in statement form, as used when computing the RTE scores.
